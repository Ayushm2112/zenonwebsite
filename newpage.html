Infosys Private Limited

Infosys Private Limited (formerly Infosys Technologies Limited) is an Indian multinational corporation that provides business consulting, information technology and outsourcing services. It has its headquarters in Bengaluru, India. Infosys is the second-largest Indian IT services company by 2016 revenues. On January 12, 2017, its market capitalisation was $34.38 Billion. In 1981, seven engineers started Infosys Limited with just US$250. From the beginning, the company was founded on the principle of building and implementing great ideas that drive progress for clients and enhance lives through enterprise solutions.
